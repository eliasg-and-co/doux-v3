· on the format
Recorded music has been frozen since 1877.

doux is the first format where the recording responds to the world it plays in — not by generating sound with an algorithm, but by modulating what was already composed.

Every note is mine. The world controls how you hear them.

The blizzard that hit NYC on February 23rd, 2026 made this track sound unlike any other version of itself — low bit depths, slow filters, heavy autowah on the lead. The city's paralysis wrote into the music in real time.


That's not a metaphor. The L Train was delayed 18 minutes. That delay set the bit depth of the drums to 3. That actually happened.
